AI Chatbots

Learn about how AI chatbots are revolutionizing customer interactions

Overview

AI chatbots are computer programs that simulate human conversation using artificial intelligence.

Core Uses/Appeal
· Simulate user interaction (Stige et al.)
· Represent user personas for testing (King; Guinness)
· Content generation + summarization (Aleessawi & Alzubi, Cowley)
· Humanlike interface, 24/7 support
. Chatbots assist school library pages (Cowley) in delivering content

ChatGPT

ChatGPT used in editorial settings to simulate dialogue and feedback (Aleessawi & Alzubi)

Advantages

ChatGPT excels in multi-turn dialogue and can retain context over
extended sessions, making it highly effective for more natural
and intelligent conversation.
. Easily customizable for different tones, personas, or industries,
allowing brands to tailor the chatbot's behavior.
· Can generate, rephrase, or summarize content on-the-fly,
enhancing user engagement through smart, context-aware
responses.
. Used in onboarding, support, and training scenarios, where it
simulates both users and assistants with a high level of linguistic
fluency.
· APIs allow seamless integration into websites and content
lowering technical barriers for deployment.
. CGPT community built a code companion to help users keep
working and efficient code across their site: https://chatgpt.com/g/g-WOATA9rAK-code-companion . It also offers a module called Coding Assistant that will code for you and/or clean up your code.

Limitations

· Chatbots can perpetuate bias, lack nuance (Aleessawi & Alzubi)
. Overuse risks trust and ethical missteps
. May mislead if not transparent about being Al
