AI Chatbots

Learn about how AI chatbots are revolutionizing customer interactions

Overview

AI chatbots are computer programs that simulate human conversation using artificial intelligence.

Core Uses/Appeal
· Simulate user interaction (Stige et al.)
· Represent user personas for testing (King; Guinness)
· Content generation + summarization (Aleessawi & Alzubi, Cowley)
· Humanlike interface, 24/7 support
. Chatbots assist school library pages (Cowley) in delivering content

ChatGPT

ChatGPT used in editorial settings to simulate dialogue and feedback (Aleessawi & Alzubi)

Advantages

ChatGPT excels in multi-turn dialogue and can retain context over
extended sessions, making it highly effective for more natural
and intelligent conversation.
· Easily customizable for different tones, personas, or industries,
allowing brands to tailor the chatbot's behavior.
· Can generate, rephrase, or summarize content on-the-fly,
enhancing user engagement through smart, context-aware
responses.
· Used in onboarding, support, and training scenarios, where it
simulates both users and assistants with a high level of linguistic
fluency.
· APIs allow seamless integration into websites and content
lowering technical barriers for deployment.
· CGPT community built a code companion to help users keep
working and efficient code across their site: https://chatgpt.com/g/g-WOATA9rAK-code-companion
· It also offers a module called Coding Assistant that will code for you and/or clean up your code.

Limitations

· Chatbots can perpetuate bias, lack nuance (Aleessawi & Alzubi)
· Overuse risks trust and ethical missteps
· May mislead if not transparent about being Al

Gemini

Gemini is a multimodal Al chatbot by Google that can process text, image, code, and documents, with tight integration into Google Workspace (Docs, Gmail, Sheets).
· Built for broad application across design planning, user simulation, live support, and content generation.
. Currently integrated with Google Cloud, Firebase, and Vertex Al, making it useful for web and app development
ecosystems.

Advantages

. Multimodal capability: Can analyze screenshots or mockups alongside text, useful in UI/UX review workflows.
. Native integration with Google tools: Seamlessly works with
Docs, Sheets, and Gmail - perfect for teams using Google
Workspace.
. Code-aware features: Gemini Pro 1.5 supports code
generation and analysis, including CSS/HTML/JavaScript
assistance.
· Better document understanding: Able to ingest large
documents (up to 1M tokens in Pro version), useful for parsing
design briefs or site maps.
· Real-time Google search access: Unlike ChatGPT (outside of
plugins or browsing mode), Gemini can access and
summarize live web content.
. Design brainstorming & copywriting: Excellent for quick
content ideas, tagline testing, or product description drafting.

Limitations

. Still maturing in conversational nuance: Compared to
ChatGPT, Gemini can be less refined in long, multi-
turn dialogues.
· Limited plugin ecosystem: Gemini lacks the depth of
ChatGPT's third-party plugin integrations (like Zapier,
Canva, etc.).
. Fewer customization options: No easy way (yet) to
fine-tune its personality or memory settings for
brand-specific tone.
. Less community adoption: Fewer developer guides
and prebuilt integrations compared to the more
mature ChatGPT ecosystem.
. Google dependency: Works best inside the Google
ecosystem - outside of it, capabilities drop off.
